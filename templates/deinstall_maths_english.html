Before deinstalling software, please follow these instructions.

Backup existing scores

  1. Upload your scores.

    Start > Programs > The Interactive Classroom > Scores Upload

    Some image

  2. Click on the Backup Scores button.

    Some image

  3. Then click on the Change button.

    Some image

  4. Pick a location. From the screen that appears, choose Drive and then select either your USB Disk or your Floppy Drive (e.g., A:) to copy the scores to.

    Some image

  5. Click OK, and then click the Backup button.

    Some image

  6. Wait a moment until you receive this message:

    Your scores have been successfully copied.
    Some image
  7. Proceed with de-installing the software. (See below.)

Deinstalling the software

  1. Go to Start > Programs > The Interactive Classroom > Deinstall Software
    Some image

  2. Warning: Do NOT close the deinstaller dialog until you have completed ALL the steps here.

    Important: Copy the following entry code into the Computer Tutor deinstallation screen on your computer and complete the deinstallation process.
